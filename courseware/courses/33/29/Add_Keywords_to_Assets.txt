Add Keywords through the MC Admin Functionality 

1. Login to the MC Admin page: http://mediacenter.plastipak.com/index.php?site=admin

2. Go to the appropriate folder to find the recently uploaded asset. 

3. Click on the box below the image to edit. 

4. Add Keywords and click “Save Keywords”.
Note: Add "includeme" to the list of keywords (this adds it to the "Top Results" section). Use commas to separate keywords. No spaces after commas. Keywords 3 characters or less will not be recognized.
